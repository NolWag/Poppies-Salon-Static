About Us

After more than two decades in the beauty industry Lien Sweezie founded Poppies in early 2015 with a single boutique salon in Richland, Michigan. Later that same year she established her flagship location inside Crossroads Mall in Portage, Michigan. Poppies is dedicated to bringing their expertise in all aspects of beauty culture to their clients. Beauty Artistry of the highest degree is the Poppies way.
